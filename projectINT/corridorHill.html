Corridor Hill Residence

Corridor Hill is a privately-managed residence designated exclusively for students of Tshwane University of Technology (TUT). It is located off-campus but remains easily accessible to the university.

Accommodation Details:

  • Only Sharing (Double) Rooms are available.
  • No Single Rooms are offered.

Residence Rules and Amenities:

  • A shared fridge is provided in each unit. Students may also bring their own bar fridge if desired.
  • A microwave is provided; however, students may bring their own personal microwave.
  • Free transport is provided to and from the TUT campus hourly.

Residence Capacity:

  • Total Beds Available: 1600+
  • Female Students: 800
  • Male Students: 400
  • Other/Reserved Beds: Approximately 400

Corridor Hill offers a vibrant, secure environment for TUT students seeking a residence close to campus while enjoying the benefits of private accommodation and free daily transport.
